Benchmark Datasets for Sound Source Localization

Here you can find a collection of datasets for benchmarking sound source localization algorithms. For each dataset, we provide a brief description, audio and video clips for each sound instance, ground truth source locations, and other relevant metadata.

Datasets

Speaker-4M-E1

A dataset of sounds emitted by a stationary speaker at ~400 positions.

Size: 15GB

Edison-4M-E1

A dataset of sounds emitted by an Edison robot performing a random walk around Environment 1.

Size: 44GB

SoloGerbil-4M-E1

A dataset of vocalizations emitted by lone, freely-behaving adolescent gerbils in response to vocalizations presented through a speaker.

Size: 7.5GB

DyadGerbil-4M-E1

A dataset of spontaneous vocalizations emitted by various pairings of two gerbils.

Size: 86MB

GerbilEarbud-4M-E1

A dataset of sounds emitted by an earbud affixed to the head of a freely-behaving adult gerbil.

Size: 1.3GB

Hexapod-8M-E2

A dataset of sounds emitted by an ultrasonic speaker affixed to a hexapod robot walking in parallel lines across Environment 2.

Size: 236GB

MouseEarbud-24M-E3

A dataset of sounds emitted by an earbud affixed to the head of a lone, freely-behaving mouse.

Size: 208GB

SoloMouse-24M-E3

A dataset of vocalizations emitted by a freely-behaving mouse.

Size: 362MB

DyadMouse-24M-E3

A dataset of vocalizations emitted by two freely-behaving mice.

Size: 2.1GB

Environments

Environment 1

A prismic environment with hard plastic walls, acoustic foam lining the ceiling, and an inch of bedding on the floor.

Environment 2

A prismic environment with hard plastic for its walls and ceiling and a layer of bedding on its floor.

Environment 3

A square environment with acoustically transparent walls and a Lexane floor.

Speaker-4M-E1

  • A dataset of sounds emitted by a stationary speaker at ~400 positions.

  • Stimuli include 5 noise-free gerbil vocalizations and a white noise stimulus, each presented at three volume levels.

  • The speaker's positions lie along a square grid with ~2cm spacing.

  • # samples: 70,914

  • # stimulus classes: 18

  • Sampling rate: 125kHz

  • # Microphones: 4

  • Environment: Environment 1

  • Size: 15GB

Edison-4M-E1

  • A dataset of sounds emitted by an Edison robot performing a random walk around Environment 1. The stimuli are gathered from longitudinal recordings of gerbil families [Peterson et al. 2023. eLife] and presented through an earbud. The dataset includes ground truth locations for the earbud and the robot’s orientation.

  • # samples: 266,877

  • # unique stimuli: 10,049

  • # microphones: 4

  • Sampling rate: 125kHz

  • Environment: Environment 1

  • Size: 44GB

SoloGerbil-4M-E1

  • A dataset of vocalizations emitted by lone, freely-behaving adolescent gerbils in response to vocalizations presented through a speaker.

  • # samples: 61,513

  • # microphones: 4

  • Sampling rate: 125kHz

  • Environment: Environment 1

  • Size: 7.5GB

DyadGerbil-4M-E1

  • A dataset of spontaneous vocalizations emitted by various pairings of two gerbils.

  • Pairings: male-male, female-female, male-female, mother-pup

  • # samples: 653

  • # microphones: 4

  • Sampling rate: 125kHz

  • Environment: Environment 1

  • Size: 86MB

GerbilEarbud-4M-E1

  • A dataset of sounds emitted by an earbud affixed to the head of a freely-behaving adult gerbil. The stimuli are gathered from longitudinal recordings of gerbil families [Peterson et al. 2023. eLife].

  • # samples: 7,698

  • # microphones: 4

  • Sampling rate: 125kHz

  • Environment: Environment 1

  • Size: 1.3GB

Hexapod-8M-E2

  • A dataset of sounds emitted by an ultrasonic speaker affixed to a Freenove hexapod robot walking in parallel lines across Environment 2. As it walks, the arm carrying the speaker is rotated to different orientations to vary the directionality of the emitted sounds throughout the dataset. The stimuli are gathered from longitudinal recordings of gerbil families [Peterson et al. 2023. eLife].

  • # samples: 156,900

  • # unique stimuli: 100

  • # microphones: 8

  • Sampling rate: 192kHz

  • Environment: Environment 2

  • Size: 236GB

MouseEarbud-24M-E3

  • A dataset of sounds emitted by an earbud affixed to the head of a lone, freely-behaving mouse. The stimuli are noise-filtered mouse vocalizations. Ground truth locations include elevation (position along z-dimension), but not orientation.

  • # samples: 200,000

  • # microphones: 24

  • Sampling rate: 250kHz

  • Environment: Environment 3

  • Size: 208GB

SoloMouse-24M-E3

  • A dataset of vocalizations emitted by a freely-behaving mouse.

  • # samples: 549

  • # microphones: 24

  • Sampling rate: 250kHz

  • Environment: Environment 3

  • Size: 362MB

DyadMouse-24M-E3

  • A dataset of vocalizations emitted by two freely-behaving mice.

  • # samples: 2,191

  • # microphones: 24

  • Sampling rate: 250kHz

  • Environment: Environment 3

  • Size: 2.1GB

Environment 1

  • A prismic environment with hard plastic walls, acoustic foam lining the ceiling, and an inch of bedding along the floor. Four microphones are mounted at the ceiling’s corners and directed at the center of the floor.

  • # microphones: 4

  • Microphone model: Avisoft-Bioacoustics CM16/CMPA48AAF-5V

  • Sampling rate: 125kHz

  • Dimensions (m):

    • Top: 0.616 x 0.413

    • Bottom: 0.559 x 0.356

    • Height: 0.368

Environment 2

  • A prismic environment with hard plastic for its walls and ceiling and a layer of bedding on its floor.

  • # microphones: 8

  • Microphone model: Avisoft-Bioacoustics CM16/CMPA48AAF-5V

  • Sampling rate: 192kHz

  • Dimensions (m):

    • Top: 1.218 x 0.914

    • Bottom: 1.218 x 0.914

    • Height: 0.610

Environment 3

  • A square environment with acoustically transparent walls and a Lexane floor. Twenty-four microphones line the perimeter, roughly 1 cm above the floor. Each microphone is perpendicular to the wall it borders.

  • # microphones: 24

  • Microphone model: Avisoft-Bioacoustics CM16/CMPA

  • Sampling rate: 250kHz

  • Dimensions (m):

    • Top: 0.615 x 0.615

    • Bottom: 0.615 x 0.615

    • Height: 0.425
